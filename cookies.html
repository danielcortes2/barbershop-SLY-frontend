Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device (computer, tablet, mobile) when you visit them. They help make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Technical Cookies (Necessary)

These are essential for the proper functioning of the website. Without them, you may not be able to use some features correctly.

Preference Cookies

These allow the website to remember information so you don't have to enter it again each time you visit (e.g., preferred language).

Analytical Cookies

These help us understand how visitors interact with our website, providing information about areas visited, time spent, and any issues encountered.
